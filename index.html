Dr. Abhishek Kushwaha, Ph.D.

Post-Doctoral Fellow at Lehrstuhl für Optische Messverfahren für die Energie- und Verfahrenstechnik (LOM)

RWTH Aachen University, Germany

Current

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Lehrstuhl für Optische Messverfahren für die Energie- und Verfahrenstechnik (Chair of Optical Diagnostics in Energy, Process and Chemical Engineering) for short LOM in Rheinisch-Westfälische Technische Hochschule (RWTH) Aachen University, Germany.

M.S. and Ph.D.

I have attained both my M.S. and Ph.D. degrees, from the Department of Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras, located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. My research focused on thermoacoustic instability in hydrogen-enriched methane combustion. Throughout my doctoral journey, I was guided by Dr. R. I. Sujith.

Bechalors

I received my Bachelors in Aeronautical Engineering from Babu Banarasi Das, National Institute of Technology and Management, Lucknow, India where I was advised by Prof. C.C. Gupta.
